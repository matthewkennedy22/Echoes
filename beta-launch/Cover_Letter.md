**Subject:** Inviting your museum to help build a San Luis Obispo history project

Dear Director,

My name is **Matthew Kennedy**, a Cal Poly MSBA alumnus and six-year resident of San Luis
Obispo. I'm building a project called **ECHOES**, a way for people to have a real,
source-grounded conversation with figures from our county's history. The first figure is
**Myron Angel**, the pioneer journalist, county historian, and father of Cal Poly.

ECHOES lets a visitor ask Myron a question and hear him answer in his own 1905 voice, but
**only from real, validated historical sources**, with the evidence shown for every claim
and an honest "I don't have a source for that" when it doesn't know. He can even show
historic photographs of the Mission, Morro Rock, old downtown, and the early Polytechnic.

I'm reaching out because **I'd like your organization to be a beta partner.** It's free to
use during the beta; you would cover only the small AI usage cost, typically just a few
dollars a month, and in return you'd help shape the product with your feedback and
expertise. My goals are simple: **to promote history learning in San Luis Obispo, and to
continue my research into how AI can be made accurate and trustworthy for education.**

I've enclosed a one-page overview that explains how ECHOES works and what partnership
involves. You can also **try the live beta now**:

**https://echoes-inky-zeta.vercel.app/**

Could I show you a **15-minute live demo**, in person or by phone, at a time
that's convenient for you? I'm happy to work around your schedule.

Thank you for everything you do to preserve our county's history. I'd be honored to build
this alongside you.

# ECHOES Beta Launch Kit (San Luis Obispo history partners)

Ready-to-send materials for inviting San Luis Obispo County's **historical societies,
museums, libraries, and archives** to join the ECHOES beta, featuring **Myron Angel**,
the father of Cal Poly and county historian.

**Prepared by:** Matthew Kennedy, Cal Poly MSBA alumnus & six-year SLO resident
matthewkennedy22@gmail.com · (925) 285-2090

**Purpose of this outreach (two goals):**
1. **Promote history learning** in San Luis Obispo with a trustworthy, engaging tool.
2. **Advance research** into accurate, source-grounded conversational AI.

## What's in this folder

| File | What it's for | When to use it |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| `ECHOES_Beta_Pamphlet.md` | The one-page overview (how it works, the offer, the vision). | The handout: print it or attach it to an email. |
| `Cover_Letter.md` | Finished intro letter / email to send with the pamphlet. | First contact, in person or by email. |
| `Beta_Partner_FAQ.md` | Detailed Q&A (accuracy, cost, rights, privacy, the catch). | For partners who want specifics before saying yes. |
| `Beta_Partner_Agreement.md` | Plain-language outline of what each side commits to, with a sign-off. | When a partner is ready to start. |

All documents are **finished and ready to send** with contact details included. Include
`matthew-kennedy.jpg` in the same folder when emailing or printing (it appears next to
contact info in each document).

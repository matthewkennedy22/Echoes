# ECHOES Beta Partnership Outline

A plain-language summary of what each side agrees to during the beta. This is meant to set
clear expectations, not to serve as a heavy legal contract.

---

## The short version

**Your organization** agrees to try ECHOES and share feedback. **ECHOES (Matthew Kennedy)**
provides the chatbot free of charge during the beta, with your organization covering only
small at-cost AI usage. Either side may end the beta at any time.

---

## What ECHOES provides

- **Free access** to the ECHOES chatbot (Myron Angel) for the duration of the beta.
- A simple **web link and/or QR code**, with no installation or IT work required.
- **Live demo URL:** https://echoes-inky-zeta.vercel.app/
- All technical work, setup, maintenance, and updates.
- **Evidence-labeled, source-grounded** answers with visible citations.
- Use of **only public-domain, partner-owned, or permissioned** source material.
- **Partner approval** of any content drawn from your collection before it is used.
- Transparency on AI usage costs and the ability to set a **monthly spending cap**.

## What the partner provides

- A willingness to **use ECHOES and give honest feedback** (accuracy, gaps, usefulness).
- Optional: **suggestions** of sources, photographs, topics, or future figures.
- Optional: a **short testimonial or case-study quote** if the experience is positive.
- Payment of the **at-cost AI usage** (see below).

## Costs

- **No licensing or subscription fee during the beta.**
- The partner covers **AI usage at cost**, typically a fraction of a cent per
  conversation; for most partners just a few dollars a month even with steady use.
- A **monthly cap** can be set in advance. The partner is never charged above the cap.
- No markup is added during the beta.

## Content, rights & accuracy

- ECHOES uses only **public-domain, partner-owned, or explicitly permissioned** material.
- The partner **retains all rights** to its own materials; nothing here transfers ownership.
- ECHOES is an **AI simulation** and may contain errors; the partner is **not responsible**
  for the chatbot's outputs, and ECHOES will act on accuracy feedback promptly.

## Privacy

- The public uses ECHOES **without creating accounts.**
- Data collection is kept minimal; retention specifics can be tailored to the partner.

## Term & exit

- The beta is **month-to-month and informal.** Either party may pause or end participation
  **at any time, for any reason,** with no penalty.

## The roadmap (what the beta builds toward)

- **Now:** text + read-aloud voice conversation with historic images.
- **Next:** richer voice and expanded San Luis Obispo figures and topics.
- **Goal:** a **living portrait,** a moving picture frame of Myron you speak with face to
  face. Beta partners help shape this direction.
